## Templates Best Practices

### Email Timing
- Pre-launch: 1 week before
- Launch day: 12:01am PST (right at launch)
- Follow-up: Next day (afternoon)
- Newsletter: 3 days after launch
- Re-engagement: 2 weeks after
- Thank you: 1 month after

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Include emoji for personal emails (not influencer outreach)
- Be specific about the ask
- Create urgency for launch day

### Body Tips
- Get to the point in first 2 sentences
- Use bullet points for scanning
- Include clear CTA
- Keep under 200 words for outreach
- Can be longer for newsletter

### Follow-Up Protocol
- No response after 3 days: gentle follow-up
- No response after 7 days: move on
- If they upvote/support: personal thank you
- Track responses in spreadsheet

### Personalization Variables
- [Name]
- [Specific thing about them]
- [Day], [Date]
- [Product Hunt URL]
- [X] (numbers to update)
